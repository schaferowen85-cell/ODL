Evidence

Learn

It scales

Two minutes. Or six months. Same four steps.

HELP holds whether the conversation is two minutes in the hallway or six months across a turnaround. The four steps don’t change. The depth does.

2-minute exchange
Hallway question · 1:1 escalation
Hear them out. Surface what’s evidence vs assumption. Ask one Learn question. Agree on one next move.
60-minute meeting
Weekly forecast call · 1:1 with a rep
Open with Hear. Spend half the time on Evidence. Use Learn to draw the pattern from the team, not the manager. Proceed with one decision per topic.
Day-long offsite
Exec team session · Board reset
Hear the full room. Pressure-test every claim through Evidence. Run Learn on what the team has already tried. Land Proceed on three decisions, not thirty.
Six-month engagement
Embedded turnaround · Coaching arc
Same four steps, applied weekly until the team runs the cadence themselves. By month six, HELP is the operating system. I leave. It runs.
Who uses HELP

Different roles. Same operating system.

Sales managers

Run forecasts that hold. Coach reps through the conversation, not at them.

AEs

Handle objections without losing the deal. Move from selling to advising.

CS leaders

Keep accounts when the relationship is wobbling. Diagnose churn before it lands.

Execs

Make the call when the data is incomplete and the room is tense.
