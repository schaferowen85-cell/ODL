The HELP Operating System.

Hear · Evidence · Learn · Proceed
Step 01
Hear

Full attention, not partial input. Skip it and you train the team to escalate just to be heard.

Step 02
Evidence

Separate what you know from what you assume. The diagnosis cracks at the spot you treated a story as a fact.

Step 03
Learn

Recognition over instruction. The team gets sharper when they pull the pattern from their own past, not yours.

Step 04
Proceed

Surface the options. Do not name the winner. The team gets a decision either way. Only one version gets conviction.

The same four steps hold whether the conversation is two minutes in the hallway or a six-month turnaround. The depth changes. The steps do not. Run it until the team runs the cadence without you.
