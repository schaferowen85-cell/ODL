My philosophy

I work with teams that have the skills. They just need help uncovering the answer.

Most companies I work with already have what they need. The talent is in seat. The product works. The market is real. What’s broken isn’t the people. It’s the system around them. The cadence. The decision rights. The conversations that aren’t happening. The conversations that are happening but going nowhere.

My job is to help your team see what they can’t see from inside the work, and to give them an operating system for working through it together. I’m not the smartest person in your room. I’m the one who can hear what your team is saying, frame what they’re actually trying to solve, and keep them moving when they would otherwise stall.

That’s HELP. Hear, Evidence, Learn, Proceed. It’s how I run every meeting, every coaching session, every turnaround call.

How we work together

Three engagement models.

Model 01

Embedded turnaround.

Short-term, full-time interim role. I step in as your fractional CRO, interim revenue leader, or interim President depending on what the company needs, and I work alongside your executive team every day.

This is the right fit when
  • Revenue is breaking and you need a credible operator in the seat now
  • Your PE board needs to see decisive action
  • You’re between leaders and need continuity, not a search firm
  • You need someone who can stabilize the team, fix the GTM motion, and hand off cleanly to a permanent successor
What you get
  • Daily presence with your executive team
  • Direct accountability for GTM execution
  • A clear definition of “done” so the engagement ends on time
  • A documented operating system your team keeps after I leave
Model 02

Executive team coaching.

Part-time engagement. Weekly or biweekly cadence. I coach your CEO, CRO, founders, or full exec team on the operating decisions that are stuck.

This is the right fit when
  • You have the right people in seat but the team isn’t moving in sync
  • Specific decisions keep stalling or getting revisited
  • You want outside pattern recognition without bringing in a full-time outsider
  • Your team needs the HELP Operating System installed and reinforced over time
What you get
  • Recurring sessions with your CEO, exec team, or specific function leaders
  • Live coaching on the decisions in front of you, not theoretical frameworks
  • Asynchronous availability between sessions for time-sensitive calls
  • Minimum 3-month engagement to give the work time to compound
Model 03 · Extreme Mentoring 101

One-on-one mentoring.

One executive, complete access. I become the person you think out loud with while you change momentum, reset culture, and stand up a new go-to-market motion. Visible to your team or private to you alone.

This is the right fit when
  • You carry the hardest decisions alone and have no one inside to fully open up to
  • You’re a new CEO or founder who can’t look uncertain in front of the team
  • You’re leading a transition you can feel but can’t fully name yet
  • You want a thinking partner who has already crossed a change this size
What you get
  • Roughly 15 to 20 hours a month, 5 to 6 a week, shaped around your calendar
  • Half-day intensives or weekly check-ins, whichever the moment needs
  • Direct access between sessions for the calls that can’t wait
  • Public-figure or private-figure mode, your call

Scope and pricing

Every engagement is priced to the scope and the stakes.

Every situation is different. A PE-backed turnaround with a quarter to fix or sell isn’t the same as a founder-led team that needs coaching through a Series B build-out. I scope each engagement on the call.

What’s consistent
  • Embedded turnarounds are structured as a fixed monthly retainer with a defined start and end
  • Coaching engagements are priced per quarter, minimum 3 months
  • Every engagement has a written scope: what we’re solving, what “done” looks like, and how we measure it
  • No long-term lock-ins. If it’s not working, you can end the engagement at the end of any month.
