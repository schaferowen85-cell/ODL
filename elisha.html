Meet Elisha

Elisha Schafer. Co-founder. Co-author of HELP.

Elisha co-founded OnDemand Leaders. She brings the behavioural-science discipline behind the HELP Operating System and the trained eye for what is happening underneath what a team is saying.

The work under the work.

Chris diagnoses the system. Elisha diagnoses the dynamics. Together they built HELP — the four-step operating system for the conversations that drive every decision.

Elisha trained at McMaster and brings a clinical foundation to her work with leadership teams. She runs Lotus Counselling alongside her contribution to OnDemand Leaders, and her practice informs the parts of HELP that most consultants get wrong: how people actually hear each other, where assumptions calcify, and what it takes to surface a real decision.
